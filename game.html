Game Rules

  • 🌳 Tree: -2 Pollution, +2 Happiness, -1 Energy
  • 🏞️ Park: -5 Pollution, +5 Happiness, -2 Energy
  • 🏢 Building: +5 Pollution, -3 Happiness, +10 Population, -2 Energy
  • 🚗 Car: +3 Pollution, -2 Happiness, +5 Population, -1 Energy
  • ☀️ Solar Panel: -3 Pollution, +2 Happiness, -2 Energy
  • ⚡ Every action consumes Energy. Game ends when energy = 0.
  • 🎯 Win Condition: Pollution ≤ 0 and Happiness ≥ 80
  • 💀 Game Over: Happiness ≤ 0 or Energy = 0 without winning
